# Staff Notes (INTERNAL — do not share with customers)

## Desk Assignment

- **Desk 1 (Cassa 1)**: Primary intake — new customers, product questions, tasting requests
- **Desk 2 (Cassa 2)**: Checkout, machine servicing, returns processing

## AI Sentiment Flags

If the Kyros system flags a customer with **sentiment: negative** (score -1), the Control Room session card shows a ⚠️ warning. Greet these customers with extra patience. Common frustrations:
- Long wait times (especially Saturday mornings)
- Capsule compatibility confusion (Riserva format vs. third-party)
- Machine warranty claims or repair ETAs

## Common Edge Cases

- **Capsule compatibility**: Always verify format against the compatibility chart on the staff tablet. Do NOT promise compatibility without checking.
- **Machine repair ETA**: Standard turnaround with service partner is 10–15 business days. Do not quote shorter times.
- **Discount requests**: Riserva Card holders receive 10% on machine purchases. No ad-hoc discounts without manager approval.
- **Media / press inquiries**: Redirect to info@riservaespresso.it — do not comment on company matters.
- **Out-of-stock blends**: Check the back-room stock list (updated daily by opening staff). If truly OOS, offer subscription pre-order via website.

## Pilot Demo

This store is running the **Kyros AI Concierge Pilot**. Customers using the Kyros app see an AI chat assistant while waiting. If asked: *"It's our new digital assistant — feel free to use it while you wait!"*. Do not mention Anthropic, Claude, or any specific AI technology.
