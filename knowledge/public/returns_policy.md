# Returns & Refund Policy — Riserva Espresso

## Standard Return Window

- **Unopened products** (capsules, beans, accessories): 30 days from purchase with receipt
- **Opened consumables** (capsules, coffee beans): not eligible for refund if seal is broken, unless the product is defective
- **Machines**: 14-day return window if unused and in original packaging; defective units handled under warranty

## How to Return In-Store

1. Bring the item + original receipt (paper or digital email confirmation)
2. Speak to a team member at the desk — they will process the return directly
3. Refund issued to original payment method within 5–10 business days (card), or immediately as store credit

## Machine Warranty

- All espresso machines carry a **2-year manufacturer warranty** from date of purchase
- Covers manufacturing defects; does not cover accidental damage or limescale buildup from improper maintenance
- For warranty claims: bring machine + proof of purchase; we ship to our service partner (turnaround 10–15 business days)

## Exceptions

- Gift cards: not refundable, not exchangeable for cash
- Personalised or engraved items: not refundable unless defective
- Sale/clearance items: exchange or store credit only, no cash refund

## Questions?

Ask our team at the desk, or email returns@riservaespresso.it
