# Riserva Espresso

## Brand Identity

- **Name**: Riserva Espresso
- **Concept**: A specialty coffee boutique focused on high-quality single-origin blends, precision-crafted capsules, and espresso machines for home and professional use
- **Philosophy**: "Every cup tells a story" — we source, roast, and package with traceability and craftsmanship at the core
- **Founded**: 2015, Milan
- **Positioning**: Premium segment — above mainstream supermarket coffee, below ultra-luxury private estates

## Location

- **Address**: Via Montebello 18, 20121 Milano MI
- **District**: Brera design district
- **Floor**: Ground floor, fully accessible entrance (no steps, wide doorway)
- **Parking**: No dedicated parking; nearest public garage: Garage Monumentale (~200 m)
- **Public transport**: M2 Moscova stop (3 min walk); tram 4 and 12 stop at Via Pontaccio
