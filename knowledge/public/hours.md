## Holiday Hours

- **Christmas Eve (Dec 24)**: 10:00 – 16:00
- **Christmas Day (Dec 25)**: Closed
- **New Year's Day (Jan 1)**: Closed
- **Easter Sunday**: Closed
- **Ferragosto (Aug 15)**: Closed; reduced hours Aug 12–19 (10:00–16:00)

## Peak Hours (Expect Longer Waits)

- Weekday lunch: 12:30–14:00 (5–10 min queue)
- Saturday morning: 10:00–12:00 (busiest slot of the week)

## Notes

- Last ticket issued 15 minutes before closing
- Queue may stop earlier if the counter is at capacity
