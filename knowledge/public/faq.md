# FAQ — Riserva Espresso

## Capsules & Compatibility

**Are your capsules compatible with other machines?**
Riserva capsules use our proprietary format and are compatible only with our Presto, Maestro Pro, and Lungo Travel machines. They do not fit third-party machines.

**Do you sell capsules for other brands' machines?**
No — we only sell Riserva-format capsules.

**Can I try a blend before buying?**
Yes. We offer complimentary in-store tastings on most blends. Just ask our team when you reach the desk.

## Orders & Subscriptions

**Do you offer a subscription?**
Yes — via our website (www.riservaespresso.it). Choose frequency (weekly / bi-weekly / monthly), blend, and quantity. Pause or cancel anytime online.

**Can I place an in-store order for home delivery?**
Yes. Our team can place an online order on your behalf at the desk.

## Machines

**How often should I descale my machine?**
Approximately every 200 brewing cycles or every 2–3 months with average use. Your machine's indicator light will alert you when descaling is due.

**Do you repair machines you didn't sell?**
No — warranty and repair services apply only to machines purchased at Riserva Espresso.

**What is the return window for a machine?**
14 days if unused and in original packaging. See [[returns_policy]] for full details.
