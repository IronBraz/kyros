# In-Store Services — Riserva Espresso

## 1. Coffee Tasting (Free, Walk-In)

- Complimentary tasting of up to 3 blends at our espresso bar
- Available during regular opening hours, subject to staff availability
- No booking required — just ask at the desk

## 2. Tasting Workshops (Booking Required)

- **"Blend Journey"** — 60-minute guided tasting of 6 coffees (single-origin + blended)
  - Groups of 2–8 people
  - Includes pairing notes and take-home tasting card
  - Book via website or call the store

- **"Home Barista"** — 90-minute hands-on session on the Barista Elite machine
  - Learn: tamping, extraction timing, milk texturing
  - Max 4 participants per session
  - Includes a one-bag purchase voucher

Workshops run on selected Saturday mornings and Wednesday evenings. Calendar at www.riservaespresso.it/events.

## 3. Machine Maintenance

- **Descaling service**: bring your Riserva machine in; we descale and run diagnostics while you wait (~20 min)
- **Cleaning check-up**: free of charge for machines purchased at our store
- **Technical assessment**: for issues beyond cleaning — we ship to our service partner (see [[returns_policy]])

## 4. Gift Services

- Curated gift box assembly in-store (ready next business day)
- Personal engraving on selected cups and canisters (3–5 business days)
- Corporate gifting: minimum 10 units — contact b2b@riservaespresso.it

## 5. Loyalty Programme

- **Riserva Card**: earn 1 point per €1 spent in-store or online
- 100 points = €5 reward voucher, applied at checkout
- Double points on workshops and machine purchases
- Sign up at the desk or via the website
